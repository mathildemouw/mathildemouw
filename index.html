Hats and Scarves and Funky Yarn, Oh My!

I learned to knit in afterschool care as a kid, and picked it up again as an adult, inspired by Debbie Stoller's Stitch and Bitch book. I love experimenting with odd yarn and contrasting colors and textures. Due to the 3D challenge, I've made many hats and wrist warmers.

Awww, Plushies

I have made two knitted plushies: a small cat that reminds me of Hayao Miyazaki's soot sprites (maybe it's just the googley eyes), and a sushi roll; an homage to my foodie roots.

Music

I spend a lot of time drawing, crafting, singing, and making stuff. In addition to loosely-following knitting patterns (and creating original patterns!) I also write songs, many of which I have been able to perform as a singer-songwriter/guitarist. My music ranges from silly to serious to educational. Someday, I will like every genre of music. When someone asks me what kind of music I like, I will say "Every kind," and it will me true.
